Shoe-1

Our incomes are like our shoes; if too small, they gall and pinch us; but if too large, they cause us to stumble and to trip. A shoe is not only a design, but it's a part of your body language, the way you walk. The way you're going to move is quite dictated by your shoes.

an outer covering for the human foot typically having a thick or stiff sole with an attached heel and an upper part of lighter material (such as leather)
